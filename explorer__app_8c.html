◆ SPI_DEFAULT_SLAVE

#define SPI_DEFAULT_SLAVE   -1

Determines the SPI slave setting: use external (wired) or internal (soldered) device.

-1: Auto detect. 1: The intern S2PI slave (connected via adapter board). 2: The extern S2PI slave (connected via cable). 3: The experimental intern S2PI slave w/ GPIO CS (connected via adapter board). 4: The experimental extern S2PI slave w/ GPIO CS (connected via cable).
